## Overview

Side Gusset Bags feature expandable folds on both sides, allowing the bag to accommodate significantly more volume than flat or stand-up pouches of the same width. When filled, the gussets expand outward, creating a rectangular block-bottom shape that stands securely on shelves.

This format is the industry standard for bulk coffee (1kg+), pet food, protein powders, and any product sold in larger quantities.

## Why Side Gusset?

- **Maximum volume** — Holds 2-3x more than a flat pouch of the same width
- **Shelf stability** — Flat bottom with expanded gussets creates a very stable base
- **Efficient packing** — Rectangular shape stacks efficiently in shipping boxes
- **Premium look** — Large, flat front and back panels for maximum branding

## Closure Options

| Closure | Description | Best For |

## Heavy-Duty Construction

For products over 5kg, we reinforce the bag structure with:

- **Thicker laminates** — Up to 200 micron total thickness
- **Reinforced bottom seal** — Double-sealed for load bearing
- **Nylon layer** — PET/NY/PE structure for puncture resistance
- **Carry handles** — Die-cut or heat-sealed handles
