## Overview

Roll Film (also called Laminated Film or Flexible Laminate) is the raw material used on form-fill-seal (FFS) packaging machines. Instead of buying pre-made pouches, your production line forms, fills, and seals packages from roll stock — giving you maximum efficiency at high volumes.

We produce custom-printed laminated roll film for all major FFS machine brands, including Bosch, Rovema, Ishida, Triangle, and Viking Masek.

## How Roll Film Works

1. **Unwind** — Film unwinds from the roll into the FFS machine
2. **Form** — Machine forms the film into a tube or pouch shape
3. **Fill** — Product drops in from above
4. **Seal** — Top and bottom seals create the closed package
5. **Cut** — Individual packages are separated and discharged

## Material Structures

| Structure | Application | Features |
|-----------|------------|----------|
| PET/PE | Dry snacks, bakery | Good clarity, standard barrier |
| PET/AL/PE | Coffee, sensitive products | Maximum barrier |
| OPP/CPP | Crackers, confectionery | Good moisture barrier, glossy |
| PET/VMPET/PE | Chips, snacks | Metallized barrier, lighter than foil |
| MATT OPP/PE | Premium snacks | Matte finish, excellent print quality |

## Technical Specifications

We engineer every roll to match your specific machine requirements:

- **Width**: Custom up to 1200mm
- **Core Size**: 76mm (3") or 152mm (6") standard
- **Coefficient of Friction (COF)**: Controlled for consistent machine feeding
- **Seal Strength**: ≥30 N/15mm (standard), ≥40 N/15mm (heavy duty)
- **Hot Tack**: Optimized for high-speed lines
- **Print Registration**: ±0.5mm accuracy for automated cut registration
