## Overview

Flat Bottom Bags (also known as Block Bottom Bags or Box Pouches) are the premium choice for products that need exceptional shelf presence. Their 5-panel construction provides a flat, stable base and five printable surfaces — giving your brand more real estate than any other pouch format.

This format has become the gold standard in the specialty coffee industry and is rapidly expanding into tea, supplements, and gourmet foods.

## Why Flat Bottom?

Compared to stand-up pouches, flat bottom bags offer:

- **More shelf stability** — The flat rectangular base is inherently more stable than a curved gusset
- **Better space efficiency** — Rectangular shape packs tighter in shipping boxes and on shelves
- **Larger branding area** — 5 printable panels vs 3 on a typical SUP
- **Premium perception** — Consumers associate this format with higher-quality products

## Degassing Valve

For coffee roasters, we offer one-way degassing valves that allow CO₂ to escape while preventing oxygen from entering. This means:

- Coffee can be packed immediately after roasting
- No need to wait for degassing
- Fresher product reaches your customer
- Works with both whole bean and ground coffee

## Material Options

We use only food-grade materials from certified suppliers. Our most popular structures:

- **PET/AL/PE** — Maximum barrier for coffee and moisture-sensitive products
- **Kraft/AL/PE** — Natural kraft exterior for organic/artisan branding
- **PET/VMPET/PE** — Metallized barrier for mid-range protection
