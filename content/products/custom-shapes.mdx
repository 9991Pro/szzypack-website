## Overview

Custom Shape Pouches are the ultimate expression of brand creativity in flexible packaging. Using die-cut technology and custom-made forming molds, we can produce pouches in virtually any 2D profile — from a coffee bean to a dog bone, a Christmas tree to your company logo.

This format is ideal for brands that want to break away from rectangular packaging and create an unforgettable shelf presence.

## The Custom Shape Process

1. **Concept & Design** — You share your shape concept; our engineers assess feasibility
2. **3D Modeling** — We create a 3D structural model showing seam placement, gusset geometry, and fill behavior
3. **Mold Fabrication** — Custom forming molds are CNC-machined (2-4 weeks)
4. **Prototyping** — Sample pouches made on the actual production line
5. **Production** — Full production run after prototype approval

## Technical Constraints

While we can produce almost any shape, there are physical constraints:

- **Maximum footprint**: 400 x 600mm (expandable for large volumes)
- **Minimum radius**: 3mm on internal corners
- **Seal width**: Minimum 8mm seal area required
- **Aspect ratio**: Width-to-height ratio should be between 1:3 and 3:1
- **Fill opening**: Must accommodate your filling equipment nozzle

## Mold Investment

Custom molds are a one-time investment of $200–500 depending on complexity:

- **Refund policy**: Mold cost is credited back on your first reorder over $5,000
- **Mold life**: 500,000+ impressions with proper maintenance
- **Mold storage**: We store your mold indefinitely at no charge
