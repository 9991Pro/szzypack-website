## Overview

Stand Up Pouches (SUP) are our flagship product and the most versatile flexible packaging format on the market. With a bottom gusset that expands when filled, they stand upright on retail shelves — maximizing brand visibility while minimizing material usage.

We manufacture SUPs in a wide range of material structures, sizes, and finishes. Whether you need a small 50g sample pouch or a bulk 5kg bag, our production lines can accommodate.

## Material Structures

| Structure | Best For | Barrier Level |
|-----------|----------|---------------|
| PET/AL/PE | Coffee, moisture-sensitive products | High |
| PET/VMPET/PE | Snacks, dried foods | Medium-High |
| Kraft/AL/PE | Organic, natural branding | High |
| MATT OPP/CPP | Light snacks, visibility | Low-Medium |
| PET/PE | Non-sensitive dry goods | Low |

## Key Features

- **High Barrier Protection** — Multi-layer lamination blocks oxygen, moisture, and UV light, extending shelf life up to 24 months.
- **Resealable Zipper** — Press-to-close zipper keeps contents fresh after opening.
- **Tear Notch** — Laser-scored for clean, easy tearing without scissors.
- **Euro Slot / Hang Hole** — Optional die-cut hole for pegboard retail display.
- **Custom Shapes** — Rounded corners, contoured edges, or full die-cut shapes available.
